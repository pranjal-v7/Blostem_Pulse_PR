================================================================
  BLOSTEMPULSE — 3-MINUTE DEMO VIDEO SCRIPT
================================================================


[0:00 — INTRO]

"Hi everyone, for the Blostem Hack to the Future Hackathon, I tackled 
a major pain point for the marketing and sales teams: manual prospecting. 

Reps spend hours reading fintech news, guessing which companies are 
ready to buy, and writing cold outreach from scratch while dodging RBI compliance risks.

My solution is BlostemPulse.

It's an AI-powered platform that autonomously discovers Indian fintech
prospects, scores their buying intent using Gemini AI, and generates 
compliant outreach — with zero manual input. Let me show you."


[0:20 — LOGIN PAGE]
[Open: blostem-pulse-pr.vercel.app]

"This is the login page. On the left you can see live stats from the
platform — companies tracked, hot leads, and RBI alerts — all pulling
from the live database.

On the right, sign in with email or Google OAuth. New users fill in
their name, age, and department — and land directly on the dashboard."

[Sign in]


[0:40 — RADAR DASHBOARD]
[Now on /app/radar]

"This is the Prospect Radar — the intelligence feed.

At the top, four KPI tiles: total companies, hot leads, last scan time,
and active alerts. Next to them is the 'Discover Now' button, which
manually triggers the autonomous discovery pipeline — scanning 7 Indian
fintech RSS feeds, extracting company names with Gemini AI, validating
them via SerpAPI, and inserting them with intent scores.

Below the KPIs are macro alert pills — live RBI and SEBI regulatory
events that affect the whole pipeline. Dismissible per session.

The prospect list is sorted into three tiers: Hot, Warm, and Cold
based on AI intent score from 0 to 100.

Each card shows the company logo, sector, funding stage, city, signal
count, and the score ring. The 'Scan' button on each card triggers a
deep scan — fresh signals fetched, score recalculated, delta animates
right on the card.

The 'Scan All Live' button does this for every company at once."

[Click Scan on one card — show delta animation]


[1:20 — COMPANY DETAIL]
[Click a hot lead]

"Clicking a company opens the detail view.

Contact information — CTO email, LinkedIn, and website — fetched live
using SerpAPI and Gemini AI. No manual research.

Below that, the AI alignment analysis explains exactly why this company
has this score: what regulatory pressure they're under, what signals
drove the intent, and why the buying window is open right now.

The signal timeline shows every news headline captured for this company,
the source, and how much each signal contributed to the final score."


[1:50 — OUTREACH PAGE]
[Navigate to Outreach in sidebar]

"In the Outreach module, you select a company, pick your stakeholder —
CTO, CFO, Compliance Head, or Founder — and choose the tone.

Hit Generate Email. Gemini streams a personalised email in real time,
built around this company's actual signals and your ICP.

The platform then auto-checks it against 7 RBI, SEBI, and DPDPA
compliance rules. Any violation is flagged inline. One click
on Auto-Fix rewrites only the flagged sentences — same intent,
fully compliant. Then open directly in Gmail."

[Show streaming email, compliance check, Gmail button]


[2:30 — SETTINGS]
[Navigate to Settings in sidebar]

"Settings lets you update your profile, upload a photo, and set your
role. The theme toggle switches between dark and light mode instantly —
persisted across sessions.

The ICP editor defines what kind of company you're targeting. Saving it
triggers a full AI rescore of every prospect in the pipeline.

Scan frequency controls how often the autonomous pipeline runs —
two times a day, four times, or manual — managed by a pg_cron job
running in IST."


[2:55 — CLOSE]

"BlostemPulse handles discovery, scoring, contact research, outreach,
and compliance — automatically. The team just has the conversation.

Live at blostem-pulse-pr.vercel.app."
